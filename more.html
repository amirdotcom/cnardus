Tutorial of C.Nardus

Watch our videos for more videos!

Ever think on going DIY? don't worry here's how you can make it yourself at home!

Prepare 1L of cooking oil, 2 slices of book soap (250-300g) and 300ml of water.

Add 300ml of water into the container.

Add adjustable amount of soap.

Add the cooking oil and blend together until combined.

Let the essence rest, (compound in step 3) mentioned for 1 night.

Mix 250 ml of fragrant lemongrassm

Then, add 250g essence and mix with 250ml c.nardus water.

Spray repellent is ready to use.
